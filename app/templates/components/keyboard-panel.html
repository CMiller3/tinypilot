Your OS and browser capture certain key combinations before they reach TinyPilot (e.g., Ctrl + Alt + Del).

Press these buttons to send the corresponding modifier keys on your next keystroke. Double click them to send the modifier key immediately.

To simulate a modifier key from the right side of the keyboard (e.g., right Shift), set the toggle to "Right" before double clicking a modifier key.
